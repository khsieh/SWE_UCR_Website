Join SWE!

How to Join
To become an official SWE-UCR member there are three things you need to do:
1. Sign up at http://www.swe.org/join ($20 a year)
2. Send your name, UCR email address, preferred email address for SWE mail, major, phone number and student ID number to swe [at] engr.ucr.edu.
3. Participate!
