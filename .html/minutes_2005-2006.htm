SWE Ice Cream Social, October 11, 2005

Number of participants: 40

Everyone who came sat around and ate ice cream. After that, we also had an ice breaker called "Paper airplanes." Participants answer questions on a piece of paper, fold it into a paper airplane, and throw it across the room. Someone else finds that piece of paper and try to find the person to match the answers on the paper.
