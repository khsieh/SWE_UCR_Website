Activities

The Beat Goes On
Students will construct a simple device to measure their heart rates and copare how the rates change during different activities 		Easy Lava Lamp
Make your own custom lava lamp using simple household items and watch the checmical reactions swirl the colors 		Non-Newtonian Fluids
Is it a liquid? Or is it a solid? How can a mixture of corn starch and water be so confusing? Find out and experiment of yourself!
Plasma Soup
Find out what your blood is really made of in a completely edible plasma soup
Capillary Action
Learn about the different blood vessels in our system and how fast blood travels through each of them
Sturdy Structures 101
Take the structural engineering challege propsed by Discovery Engineering.org--Using only 10 sheets of copy paper and 10 inches of maksing tape, attempt to rase a book at least 12 inches off the ground for 30 seconds!
Cauldron Bubbles
What comes up usually comes down. Now lets see some bubbles reverse the order.
Electric Gelatin
Learn to control gelatin using static from a balloon
Color Spash
Observe the way food coloring dances on oil and find out how the two substances will never mix together
Balloon Flinker
Use the power of gravity to keep a helium balloon from rising. Experiment with different weights and see who's ideas can make the balloon float mid-air for the longest period of time
Polishing Pennies
A classic science experiment: Find out which liquids do the best job in shining different pennies from different time periods
Make a Hoop Glider
A different take on building paper airplanes. Use paper, straw and some tape to make an extraordinary gliding paper plane.
Buoyancy Boats
Compete for a prize by building a boat that can hold the most weight in water using only aluinum foil and pebbles for weights
Build your own hovercraft
Using a balloon, paper plates and old cardboard tubes, make your own hovercraft and watch it glide across tables
Gum drop Towers
Take a shot at building the world's tallest gumdrop tower!
Scented Balloons
Guess the contents of these balloons and find out why the scents were able to leak!
Make a Polymer
Make your own slime and goo using simple household materials!
Gum drop Towers
Take a shot at building the world's tallest gumdrop tower!
Lip Balm
Make your own homemade flavored lip balm with simple kitchen ingredients
Eggs and Their Environment
Find out what happens to eggs when they are exposed to different extreme conditions
DNA Extraction
Extract a string of DNA from a common strawberry!
