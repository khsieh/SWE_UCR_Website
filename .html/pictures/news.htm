SWE Joins Other BCOE Clubs in Celebrating National Engineers Week Feb 15th-21st

The National Engineers Week (E-Week) will be celebrated from Feb 15-21, 2009. SWE UCR will host a remote control car obstacle course on Thursday, Feb 19th from 11:20-2:00 PM. We encourage you to volunteer and participate!

For more information, contact our Professional Development Officer Annemarie Fata.

Participate in the LeaderShape Institute during Spring Break 2009

The LeaderShape Institute is an interactive, energizing, and unique experience that builds leadership skills no other program can match. It is six days of non-stop self-discovery and learning from practical experiences that build your leadership concepts and abilities. It's FREE!!

Applications are due by 5:00pm on Fri, February 20. Participant selection and notification will be made by February 26. For more information, follow the links below:

  • About LeaderShape
  • LeaderShape at UCR
