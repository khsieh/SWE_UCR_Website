April 4, 2011

Last week at the officer meeting we went over some events that and planning for the remainder of Spring quarter.

-Spring Training will take place at UCR (Bourns A265) from 9am-1pm on April 23. Training is important for the transition into next year especially for the new officers who want to get a feel for what their position will entail.

-SSED is taking place this Saturday, April 9. If you are volunteering, but didn't get a chance to attend the training this past Friday, contact Monique for more information.

-There will be a Space Day at Jefferson Elementary on May 20th. We need to put together an activity to present to the kids. Overall, outreach is very important this quarter due to our standing in in the region for outreach, so lets try to launch ahead to first place!

-Socials- We want to do one more social this quarter. Some ideas bounced around included kayaking, bowling tournament, BBQ, rock climbing, etc. Also, ask for feedback at the general meetings.

-Fundraising- Also, want to do one more fundraiser this quarter and continue advertising T-Shirts.

Lastly, we went over the individual responsibilities of the officer positions, which will be summarized below:

  • President: plans meetings, relays info, delagates tasks.
  • Vice President: helps President with needed tasks.
  • Treasurers: Coordinates fundraising as well as T-Shirts and manages the accounts.
  • Webmaster: Performs upkeeping on both the UCR and Facebook SWE pages.
  • Historian: Records and takes pictures at events.
  • Outreach: coordinates activities including SSED, local events, autistic camp, and possibly next year, more things with SWE-OC.
  • Socials: Plan social activities.
  • Secretary: Takes notes at all meetings and email to necessary people, clean out gmail account and help with Quarterly Reports.
Thanks for taking the time to read and see you soon! Lauren Richards

October 13, 2010

The main focus of the Officer meeting on October 13 was to discuss upcoming goals for the different officers.

Treasurers- Come up with two fundraisers including, but not limited to, TV show viewings, restaurants, car wash, etc. Ask Anna if you need any help/advice.

Social Chairs- Bowling social this Saturday at CSUF-arrange carpools for those that replied to email. Come up with ideas for future social events-perhaps something Halloween related.

Webmaster-Update the website with upcoming events.

Professional Development- Keep updated on fashion show.

Outreach- Monique is going to start talking about SSED at the upcoming general meeting to get people who are interested in serving on a committee and helping out with the planning. We need more people since a goal this year is to include a high school age component, which would require extra work and adequate planning. The necessary committees include Publicity, Registration, Signs/T-Shirts/Food, Activities and Speakers. As far as funding, there are Program Development Grants through SWE that can be applied for which are focused on the area of outreach. On other outreach news, there is a contact email in the swe gmail account about working with the autistic kids on a Theater Night and other events. Contact them to get plans going. Also, organize a food/present drive for Christmas.

It is possible for there to be a lot of crossover in the upcoming events as far as turning the fashion show into a potential social/fundraising event as well as thinking about fundraising during SSED with food.

There is a tutoring/ mentoring program in the works with SAE to help underclassmen. It would be pretty relaxed in terms of volunteering time to be in a specific room or lab and help whoever stops by.

For any of these events, advertize through Jun if needed and check with Heather as far as scheduling.

Also , ALL OFFICERS NEED TO REGISTER THROUGH SWE NATIONAL FOR US TO BE IN GOOD STANDING. If you have not done so already, register through swe.org.
