Posted 02/06/2007 by Megan
SWE-UCR Selected to Host "WOW! That's Engineering?"

The Society of Women Engineers National selected the UCR SWE student section to host their Wow! That's Engineering? outreach event in November 2007.  The event is designed to bring young girls together with female engineers to learn more about the field of engineering through interactive activities and discussions.  The selection process included a review of 22 applications from 19 professional sections, and 3 student sections across the nation.  Collaborators for the event will include SWE national, and the Orange County Professional Section to plan for the West Coast event.   The other two host sites selected were Atlanta and Minnesota.

SWE-OC Scholarships (applications due March 30th, 2007)

Undergraduate Scholarship Information:
SWE-OC will award one Undergraduate Scholarship in the amount of $1,000. The minimum criteria for selection are as follows:
• Open to women only
• Student at:
o CSU Fullerton, CSU Long Beach, UC Irvine, or UC Riverside
• Enrolled in an undergraduate degree program in an engineering field, such as Electrical, Mechanical or Civil Engineering.
• GPA of 3.0 or higher
• Plans to pursue a career in an engineering field
The recipient of this scholarship will be announced at the SWE-OC recognition banquet typically held in May. Students are also encouraged to apply for SWE’s National scholarships, which are awarded during the annual National Conference (for more information visit www.swe.org).

Campus Safety Escort Service

The Campus Safety Escort Service is safe and easy to use. The service is free!
There are four ways one can get an escort:

i. Pick up any red phone on campus that says Campus Safety Escort Service on it and it will automatically connect you to the Dispatcher Desk.
ii. Walk up to the Dispatcher Desk located inside Rivera Library and ask for an escort directly.
iii. Call the Dispatcher Desk at (951) 827-3772 and ask for an escort.
iv. If you see an escort on campus, just wave them down and they will walk you to your destination.

SWE cares about your safety!

2005 Regional Space Science and Engineering Day

Congratulations to SWE-UCR for another outstanding outreach event! From the COE news page:

"Representatives from NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL) teamed with the Bourns College of Engineering chapter of the Society of Women Engineers and the Riverside Astronomical Society to offer an afternoon of fun hands-on learning activities and presentations ranging from JPL space missions to rewarding careers in engineering...Dean Reza Abbaschian and UCR Chancellor France Córdova attended the event, and shared their enthusiasm for science and engineering with a large crowd of elementary and middle school girls and boys. Attendance was estimated at close to 1,000. Although the majority were Girl Scouts, many in their colorful uniforms, children of UCR faculty and staff were also invited to participate. The day was made possible through funding provided by Xerox Corporation."
