SWE 2004-2005 CALENDAR

- 10/15 is the deadline to have your pizza card order forms in to
Marie or Cynthia. Cards are good for "buy one get one free" at Pizza
Hut for a full year, and they are $10 each. Order as many as you like
for personal use or for resale (clubs--especially officers, sororities
and fraternities, dorms--particularly RAs, professors, and friends are
good candidates), and if you'd like an order form please contact
Cynthia (ccart001@ucr.edu).
- 10/21 SWE Orange County is hosting a dinner ($20 for SWE members)
featuring "Emissions Trading - Is It Working?", a talk given by a
Senior Manager at South Coast Air Quality Management District. Contact
swe@engr.ucr.edu for more information.
- On 11/3 from 4-7pm SWE members are invited to Northrop Grumman
Corporation's Integrated Systems facility in El Segundo for a tour and
briefing of its F/A-18 aircraft assembly line and other advanced
technologies. Due to the classified work and products generated at
this facility, attendees must be U.S. citizens and approved in advance
of the tour. Contact swe@engr.ucr.edu for more information.
